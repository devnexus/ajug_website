The Atlanta Java Users Group (AJUG) is a nonprofit organization with a mission to promote learning, exchange of information and discussion in our community on topics related to the Java programming language and platform. AJUG holds monthly meetings and welcomes all who which to attend and participate. AJUG also organizes an annual conference, https://devnexus.com/, which is the largest Java conference in the USA
